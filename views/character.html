MY LEVEL 50 CHARACTER

Living on an off-grid homestead near the ocean, I wake up without an alarm, drink local spring water and walk outside barefoot for a morning movement session — strength training, running, martial arts tricking, mobility flow, bouncing and flipping on the trampoline, or a surf session. I am ripped, freakishly strong, acrobatic, and highly flexible. Breakfast of foraged/farmed local ingredients — deeply nourishing and energizing. Then it’s off to “work!” I don’t have to work for money as I’m financially independent and/or 100% self sufficient, but I choose to tackle fun, diverse and meaningful passion-projects that serve the planet, my community and myself — permaculture and homestead design, filmmaking, language learning, music, archery, foraging, writing… with friends or alone I get lost in “flow” and actively seek growth, fulfillment and adventure. My afternoons are spent lounging in the sun with a good book, hiking/rock climbing in the mountains, cuddled by the fire as snow falls outside, or immersed in a passion-project. Evenings are spent with family and friends, outside around a fire, laughing under the stars and eating a home-cooked meal (made in our clay pizza oven). Life is aligned with nature’s rhythms — organic, stress-free, ever-changing and alive with love, energy, health and connection.

During the coldest months of the year I pack my backpack and board a plane in search of adventure, spending 3-4 months hiking volcanoes in Hawaii, camping in Patagonia, wwooffing in the Caribbean, riding motorcycles in the Himalayas, road-tripping around New Zealand… crossing epic items off my list, expanding my horizons, meeting incredible people and living a good story.
